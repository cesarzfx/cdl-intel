What is the CDL?

The League

The Call of Duty League is the premier professional esports competition for Call of Duty. Twelve city-based franchise teams compete across a season of Majors, with the top performers advancing to the World Championship.

The Format

The 25-26 season features four Majors held across the globe, followed by the League Championship. Each Major sees teams battle through group stages and bracket play to crown an event winner.

The Teams

From North America to Europe and the Middle East, the CDL brings together the world's best competitive Call of Duty players under 12 iconic franchise banners.
